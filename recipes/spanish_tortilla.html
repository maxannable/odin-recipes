Spanish Tortilla

Eggs, potatoes, and onions -the simplicity is delicious

This Spanish staple goes great for breakfast, lunch, or even dinner! Enjoy with or without onions.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Large Potatoes
  • 1 Large Onion
  • A Splash of Olive Oil
  • 5-7 medium eggs
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Chop potatoes and onions into small pieces
  3. Cook potatoes and onions in a medium sized pan with olive oil for approx. 20 minutes/until soft.
  4. Meanwhile, blend the eggs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Combine eggs with potaotes and onions and return to pan on medium heat
  6. Flip tortilla on a plate covering the pan and return uncooked side to pan
  7. Remove wehn done, approx. another 2.5 minutes, serve after resting a few minutes
