Pimientos de Padron

Small green peppers from the city of Padron, simply delcious!

These peppers are full of flavor and occasionally a kick of heat, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds Small Green Peppers (pimientos de Padron)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt to Taste

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Heat oil in large pan.
  3. Place peppers in pan and cook until lightly charred.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and serve immediatly.
